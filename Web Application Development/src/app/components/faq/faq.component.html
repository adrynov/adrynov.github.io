Frequently Asked Questions

You may have these questions in mind. Let me answer them!

  • Great design, clean code and a friendly approach?

    Yes! I am experienced in web and mobile development, and customer support.

  • What is your actual experience?

    I started as a software engineer over 15 years ago and worked on dozens of different products - from event services to calorie-counting mobile apps. No project is too small or too difficult!

  • How quickly can you build a project?

    That depends on the complexity of your project, but I will strive to scope your project efficiently and deliver a working prototype within a month. This includes design, full-stack development and a micro-backend for your testing. Procrastination is not an option!

  • What does the process look like?

    It all depends on your needs, the client. I'll start by gathering the requirements, researching and creating high fidelity designs. Next, I will create a micro-backend that will contain a subset of your test data and connect it to a pixel-perfect prototype of the front-end, such as a website or a mobile application. I will show the prototype to you for your approval and, as the product grows, will build a dedicated back-end service for it.
